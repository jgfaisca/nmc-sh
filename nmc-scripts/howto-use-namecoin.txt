encrypt wallet:
> namecoin-cli encryptwallet <mypassphrase>


lock wallet:
> namecoin-cli walletlock


unlock wallet:
> namecoin-cli walletpassphrase <mypassphrase> <timeout> 


change wallet password
> namecoin-cli walletpassphrasechange <oldpassphrase> <newpassphrase>


sign message:
> namecoin-cli signmessage <namecoinaddress> <message>


Verify a signed message:
> namecoin-cli verifymessage <namecoinaddress> <signature> <message> 


Register a name:
"name" = (string, required) the name to register

1) chek availability 

> namecoin-cli name_show <name>

> namecoin-cli name_show d/example
error code: -4
error message:
name not found: 'd/example'

2) register

> namecoin-cli name_new <name>
[
  xxxxx,   (string) the txid, required for name_firstupdate
  xxxxx    (string) random value for name_firstupdate
]

> namecoin-cli name_new d/example
[
  "15dce3e27da9c66b2371b38b5b583c55942043680c3b04ec37c8559847b8da45", 
  "097f833eb08d725e99987662026716d8f84192a6"
]

3) First update (after step 2 Wait at least 12 blocks):

Your registration is valid and publicly visible as soon as the name_firstupdate transaction is included in the block chain. This usually takes between 5 minutes and one hour. 

> name_firstupdate <name> <rand> <tx> <value> ("toaddress")

> namecoin-cli name_firstupdate d/example "097f833eb08d725e99987662026716d8f84192a6" "15dce3e27da9c66b2371b38b5b583c55942043680c3b04ec37c8559847b8da45" 'first update'

4 Other updates:
> namecoin-cli name_update <name>

> namecoin-cli name_update d/example '{"ns": ["ns0.web-sweet-web.net", "ns1.web-sweet-web.net", "ns0.xname.org"]}'
